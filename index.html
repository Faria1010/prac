Christmas Across The Lands 2018

2017 will be our 19th year of producing the 12 Hour Christmas Radio Special, and what a ride it is! Originally called "Christmas At Home", the name was changed in 2007 to reflect how global the show has become. From across the USA and Canada, many foreign countries as far away as England, Phillipines, Sri Lanka, Bonaire, Ireland, and more. Plus we are honored to donate our show to the American Forces Network-Radio!

how to get the show

To get Christmas Across The Lands, simply fill out the simple form here You will get a confirmation email immediately, and within 24 hours we will email you a contract that will have all the information you'll need. We do it this way in order to check if the show is already in your market. The show is 12 hours long (50 minutes of content per hour, 10 minutes local avails).


You get unlimited plays for the low cost of $99 (download 320kps mp3). If you desire the Flash Drive with .wav files the cost is $129.(includes shipping, USA Only)


If you have any questions just use the Contact Us page

CAL 2017 clocks are simple and straight forward, just like previous years

You determine the length of each break, keeping in mind that the show content is appox 50 minutes per hour
